THE CONTRIBUTIONS OF BHASKARA II

Contributions of bhaskara II to algebra

1.solution of quadratic equations:- bhaskara II developed a method for solving quadratic equations , which was more more efficicent than the methods used by ancient greeks.

2.Concept of zero:- he was one of the earliest mathematicians to use zero as a placeholder in its own right.

Contributions of bhaskara II to Arthmetic

1.Decimal system:- Bhaskara II used the decimal system and developed a method for calculating the squares of numbers.

2.arthmetic operations:- He wrote about arthmetic operations such as addition , subtraction , multiplication , and division , including methods for calculating squares and cubes.

Contributions of Bhaskara II to Geometry

1.Measurement of area and volumes :- Bhaskara II developed methods for measuring areas and volumes of various geometric shapes , including triangles, quadrilaterals, and solids.

2.concept of similar triangles :- he discussed the concept of similar triangles and developed a method for calculating the sides of similar triangles.Contribution of Bhaskara II to trigonometry

1.Sine Tables:- Bhaskara II developed sine tables, which were used for calculating trigonometric functions.

2.Trigonometric identities:- he discovered several trigonometric identities, including the pythagorean identity.

Bhaskar II's contribution to mathematics had a significant impact on the development of maths in india and beyond. his works were widely studied and influencial in the development of mathematics in the Middle East , Europe , and Asia.
